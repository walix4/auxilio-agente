Family-grade safety, in real time

Auxilio brings live crime data, registered sex-offender alerts, and instant emergency response into a single app that lives in your pocket — built for parents, families, and communities.

See Features

Crime Map

A live, color-coded map of incidents reported in your area — searchable, filterable, and updated as new events come in.

Sex Offender Map

See registered sex offenders within any radius of your home, school, or workplace — with automatic alerts when someone moves nearby.

Real-Time Alerts

Geofenced push notifications the moment an incident is reported near you, your kids, or any saved location — never be the last to know.

Emergency SOS

One tap dispatches your location, contacts, and incident details to local responders and your emergency network — silent if you need it to be.

Live Crime Feed

Incidents flow into your map seconds after they're reported — assault, theft, vehicle crime, and more, all geo-tagged.

Heat Map Layers

Toggle between time-of-day, crime-type, and 7-year history layers to see where risk concentrates around your neighborhood.

Crime-Type Filters

Surface only what matters to you — violent crime, property crime, vehicle theft, drug activity — with one-tap filtering.

Custom Geofence Alerts

Draw a radius around home, school, or work and get push alerts the second any qualifying incident lands inside it.

7-Year History

Scrub a timeline back across seven years of incidents to understand patterns before deciding where to live, walk, or send the kids.

Neighborhood Watch

Privately share live updates with neighbors and trusted contacts — turn your block into an always-on safety network.
